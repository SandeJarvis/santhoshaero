About Me

Hello, I’m Santhosh.

Aerospace engineer focused on aerodynamics, CFD, and conceptual design of aircraft and UAVs.

My work and interests revolve around understanding how air interacts with surfaces, shapes, and motion — applying that knowledge to improve aerodynamic efficiency and stability. Over the years, I’ve worked on CFD analysis, experimental flow studies, and conceptual design, blending physical insight with engineering judgment.

This site is my digital engineering diary — a place where I share ideas, tools, and evolving thoughts in aerodynamics, internal and external flows, and practical design approaches.
