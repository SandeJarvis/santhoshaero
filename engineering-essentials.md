# The Engineering Essentials Vault

This is a living library of the formulas and constants I use to solve problems from first principles.

### 📐 Aerodynamics
* **Lift Equation:** $L = \frac{1}{2} \rho v^2 S C_L$
* **Drag Equation:** $D = \frac{1}{2} \rho v^2 S C_D$

### 🌡️ Thermodynamics
* **Ideal Gas Law:** $PV = nRT$
* **Heat Transfer (Conduction):** $q = -kA \frac{dT}{dx}$

### 💾 Data & Logic
I use **Python** to automate these calculations across thousands of data points. For example, my scripts at the Leibniz Institute automated the monitoring of atmospheric plasma jets, ensuring 100% data repeatability.
